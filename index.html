Kyungtae (KT) Han

Sr. Principal Researcher
Toyota Motor North America R&D
InfoTech Labs

Kyungtae (KT) Han received a Ph.D. degree in Electrical and Computer Engineering from The University of Texas at Austin in 2006. Prior to joining Toyota, Dr. Han was a Research Scientist at Intel Labs and a Director at Locix Inc. His research interests include cyber-physical systems, connected and automated vehicles, personalized mobility services, and Digital Twin. He is a Senior Member of IEEE and a member of ACM and SAE.
